Brochure Writing Guide

Instructions for Creating Effective Brochures:

  1. Define your purpose -- Clearly identify what you want the brochure to achieve.
  2. Know your audience -- Tailor content and design to your target readers.
  3. Create a compelling headline -- Grab attention immediately.
  4. Use the rule of three -- Organize content into three main sections or panels.
  5. Highlight benefits -- Focus on what readers gain, not just features.
  6. Use visual hierarchy -- Make important elements stand out through size and placement.
  7. Include clear calls-to-action -- Tell readers exactly what to do next.
  8. Use high-quality images -- Visuals should support and enhance your message.
  9. Keep text concise -- Use bullet points and short paragraphs.
  10. Include contact information -- Make it easy for readers to follow up.
